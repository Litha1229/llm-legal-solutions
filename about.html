About LLM Legal Solutions

LLM Legal Solutions is an independent legal consultancy dedicated to providing professional legal document drafting, compliance support and regulatory support to individuals, entrepreneurs and businesses throughout South Africa. Our objective is to deliver practical, reliable and ethically sound solutions while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and integrity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, professional and reliable legal document drafting, compliance support and regulatory support that assists clients in meeting their legal and regulatory obligations with confidence.

Our Vision

To become South Africa's leading independent legal consultancy recognised for excellence in legal document drafting, compliance support and regulatory support.

Our Values

  • Integrity
  • Professionalism
  • Confidentiality
  • Client-Centred Service
  • Excellence

About the Founder

LLM Legal Solutions was founded by Lithalethu Mehlwana (LLB), a Bachelor of Laws graduate from the University of Fort Hare. The consultancy was established to provide professional legal document drafting, compliance support and regulatory support for individuals, entrepreneurs and businesses.

Professional Disclaimer

Lithalethu Mehlwana is not an admitted attorney or advocate of the Republic of South Africa. LLM Legal Solutions is not a law firm and does not provide legal representation, litigation services or any legal services reserved exclusively for admitted legal practitioners.
