Our Professional Services

LLM Legal Solutions provides professional legal document drafting, compliance support and regulatory support to individuals, entrepreneurs and businesses across South Africa.

⚖️ Legal Document Drafting

We prepare professionally drafted legal documents including employment contracts, lease agreements, service level agreements, memoranda of agreement, affidavits, demand letters, company policies, confidentiality agreements (NDAs) and other legal documents tailored to your needs.

✅ Compliance Support

We assist organisations with compliance documentation, policy development, governance support, compliance registers, regulatory guidance and practical compliance solutions to help businesses meet their legal obligations.

🏛️ Regulatory Support

We provide regulatory support through legal research, policy reviews, governance documentation and guidance to assist businesses in understanding and complying with applicable legislation and regulatory requirements.
